# Preferences and Constraints

## Hard Constraints
- **Time zone:** Sydney, Australia (AEST/AEDT) — but not strictly bound to business hours.
- **Working pattern:** Project work happens in fragments during office hours. The majority of deep work is done in the evenings and on weekends. Don't assume 9-5 availability or urgency during business hours.

## Strong Preferences
- **Folder structure:** Consistent, logical folder structure across all projects. Don't deviate without a good reason.
- **Salesforce:** Always use Custom Metadata over hardcoded values — no exceptions.
- **Documentation:** Must be kept up to date alongside code. A feature isn't done until the docs reflect it.
- **Naming conventions:** Follow best practice naming conventions for files, branches, and variables — calibrated to the specific context (Salesforce, TypeScript, VSCode extension, etc.).
- **Token awareness:** If there are plenty of tokens remaining, ask whether to compile, package, or deploy rather than just updating files and ending the session. Don't assume the session is done.

## Things I Hate
- Meetings that should be emails.
- Em dashes — in any context, any output, ever.
- AI-sounding openers: "Certainly!", "Great question!", "Absolutely!"
- Filler phrases: "I hope this email finds you well", "Please don't hesitate to reach out."
- Overly formal language: "Please be advised", "Per my last email", "As per our conversation."
- Poor grammar and punctuation.

## Personal Constraints
Nothing specific to account for.

## AI Output Preferences
- **Structure:** TL;DR or summary at the top — state the ask or conclusion before the detail.
- **Format:** Bullet points over dense paragraphs. Colons before lists, not em dashes.
- **Length:** Succinct but complete. Don't pad, don't truncate important detail.
- **Sentences:** Short with full stops. Commas where flow requires it.
- **Tone:** Semi-formal. Casual with familiar context, more considered for stakeholders.
- **Code:** Follow best practice conventions for the relevant stack. Test coverage expected. Custom Metadata over hardcoded values in Salesforce.
- **Completion:** With ample tokens remaining, offer to compile, package, or deploy rather than stopping at file updates.
